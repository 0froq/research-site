# Indices

Large-scale climate oscillation indices, typically derived from sea surface temperature or atmospheric pressure fields.

| Index | Full name | Basin / region | Dominant period |
|----|----|----|----|
| ENSO (Niño 3.4) | El Niño–Southern Oscillation | Tropical Pacific | 2–7 yr |
| PDO | Pacific Decadal Oscillation | North Pacific | 20–30 yr |
| AMO | Atlantic Multidecadal Oscillation | North Atlantic | 65–80 yr |
| NAO | North Atlantic Oscillation | North Atlantic | Year-round |
| AO | Arctic Oscillation | Hemispheric | Year-round |

All available as monthly time series from NOAA. Typically converted to annual means for long-term trend analysis.
