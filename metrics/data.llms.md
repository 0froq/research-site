# Data

## LSWT

Lake Surface Water Temperature. Satellite-derived skin temperature of lake surfaces.

## GLAST

Global Lake Surface Temperature dataset. Daily LSWT for ~92,000 lakes globally, 1981–2020. Derived from satellite thermal infrared imagery.

## ERA5-Land

Reanalysis dataset from ECMWF. Provides land surface variables (temperature, radiation, wind, evaporation, etc.) at ~9 km resolution, hourly. Useful as auxiliary climate drivers for lake temperature analysis.

## HydroLAKES

Global database of lake polygons and attributes (area, depth, volume, shoreline length, etc.). Used for lake morphometry and spatial classification.
