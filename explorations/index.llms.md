## Warming Acceleration

prose · draft · [log](../explorations/warming-acceleration/log/index.llms.md)

Do global lakes exhibit heterogeneous warming acceleration — or deceleration — across space and time, and what drives it?

92,245 lakes, GLAST dataset, 1981–2020.

Methods: STL decomposition, STARS regime shifts, K-means clustering, teleconnection attribution.

The question began with the global warming hiatus as a starting point, but the focus has broadened to the full spatiotemporal structure of lake warming heterogeneity.
