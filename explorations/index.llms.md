## Lake-temperature trajectory heterogeneity

[prose](../explorations/warming-acceleration/prose/is-there-a-hiatus.llms.md) · [draft](../explorations/warming-acceleration/draft/01-global-kinematics.llms.md) · [log](../explorations/warming-acceleration/log/index.llms.md)

How heterogeneous are reconstructed lake surface water temperature trajectories across time and space, and what seasonal/ice contexts characterize cooling trajectories?

92,245 lakes, GLAST dataset, 1981–2020.

Methods: raw annual and moving-window Theil–Sen trends; STL-based low-frequency PCA; descriptive PCA-score typology; modular cooling/ice diagnostics.

GLAST is a reconstructed/corrected LSWT product, not direct in-situ observation. The active work distinguishes descriptive trajectory structure from causal attribution.
