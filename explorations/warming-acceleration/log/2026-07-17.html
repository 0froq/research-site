2026-07-17

PCA interpretation correction

The previous PC2–PC3 score-plane rotation is no longer active evidence. It was selected after observing a raw warming-speed response derived from the same temperature records. Stability under grid and hold-out resampling does not make this post-hoc projection a new PCA component, independent validation, or physical process. Chapter 2 retains PC2–PC3 as a rank-exchanging secondary subspace; the rotation remains only as an audit note.

先前 PC2–PC3 score 平面旋转已退出 active evidence。它在观察到同一温度记录派生的 raw 增温速度响应后才被选出；格网与留出稳定不能把此 post-hoc 投影变成新 PCA 成分、独立验证或物理过程。Ch2 仅保留 PC2–PC3 为可交换排序的次级子空间；旋转只作为审计笔记保存。

First external-interpretation pass

Fixed spatial-balanced PCA was tested at the 573-cell scale with nested spatial-block models: geography/lake morphology; then annual ERA5-Land wind and precipitation means/trends; then JJA-minus-DJF forcing contrasts/trends. PC1 is scalar. PC2–PC3 is evaluated jointly by total squared prediction error, invariant to an orthogonal score-plane rotation. Raw long-term warming and raw warming-speed change are parallel outcomes.

固定空间平衡 PCA 在 573 格网尺度做嵌套空间 block 模型：地理/湖泊形态；再加 ERA5-Land 年风速与降水均值/趋势；再加 JJA 减 DJF forcing 对比/趋势。PC1 为单响应；PC2–PC3 以总平方预测误差联合评估，不受正交 score 平面旋转影响。raw 长期增温与 raw 增温速度变化为平行结果。

Annual wind/precipitation raises PC1 held-out \(R^2\) from 0.247–0.289 to 0.318–0.377 across three block scales. It raises raw long-term warming only 0.012–0.015, and the seasonal extension removes that small gain. PC2–PC3 joint-subspace and raw warming-speed-change held-out \(R^2\) remain near zero or become negative under forcing extensions. Thus the current panel fails the predeclared explanatory gate for secondary temporal structure. It only establishes that the available panel is insufficient.

年风速/降水将 PC1 留出 \(R^2\) 从 0.247–0.289 提升至 0.318–0.377；raw 长期增温仅提升 0.012–0.015，季节扩展后该小增益消失。PC2–PC3 联合子空间与 raw 增温速度变化的留出 \(R^2\) 接近零，或在 forcing 扩展后为负。因此当前变量面板未通过次级时间结构解释门槛；它只说明现有面板不足。

Data boundary

Current curated forcing has wind speed, precipitation, and surface pressure only. It lacks air temperature, shortwave/longwave radiation, humidity, evaporation/latent heat, runoff, snow, and independent forcing data. ERA5-Land-driven FLAKE also participates in GLAST reconstruction. No causal or heat-budget attribution is made. Future association work starts only after a physically complete, provenance-aware predictor panel is defined.

当前整理 forcing 仅含风速、降水、气压；缺气温、短波/长波辐射、湿度、蒸发/潜热、径流、积雪、独立 forcing 数据。ERA5-Land 驱动 FLAKE 还参与 GLAST 重建；当前不作因果或热收支归因。后续关联工作须先定义物理完整、注明数据链的 predictor 面板。

Bounded teleconnection-sensitivity screen

The screen does not correlate low-frequency PCA loading series with annual indices. It reuses Step 10 lake correlations between linearly detrended raw annual LSWT anomalies and predeclared annual indices, transforms lake correlations to Fisher-\(z\), aggregates them to PCA cells, then asks whether fixed PC scores improve spatial-block held-out prediction beyond geography/lake morphology. The screen fixes Niño 3.4, PDO, NAO, and AO; lags 0 and 1; at least 30 paired years; and excludes AMO. NAO and AO are treated as one correlated family.

本筛查不将低频 PCA loading 序列与年指数直接相关。它复用 Step 10：每湖 raw 年均 LSWT 线性去趋势异常与预先指定年指数的相关，Fisher-\(z\) 后汇总至 PCA 格网；再检验固定 PC 分数能否在地理/形态之外提高空间 block 留出预测。固定 Niño 3.4、PDO、NAO、AO，滞后 0/1 年，至少 30 个配对年；AMO 排除。NAO/AO 视为一个相关 family。

PC2–PC3, evaluated as a joint rank-invariant score pair, gives positive contemporaneous held-out increments for NAO and PDO at all three block scales, all three equal-area grids, and every leave-one-continent-out refit. AO independently repeats this pass at all block scales, grids, and LOCO refits, but remains a correlated replication rather than a second mechanism. For NAO, target-grid held-out \(R^2\) rises from \(-0.076\)–\(0.008\) under geography/morphology to \(0.247\)–\(0.299\) with PC2–PC3. For PDO it rises from \(0.276\)–\(0.317\) to \(0.445\)–\(0.477\). PC1 has no stable increment. Niño 3.4 passes block and grid screens but fails LOCO when North America is omitted, so is not promoted. One-year leads do not repeat the PC2–PC3 pattern.

以可换序、联合 PC2–PC3 分数评估：NAO 与 PDO 的同期敏感性，在三种 block、三种等面积格网、去除任一大洲重拟 PCA 后，均有正留出增益。AO 也独立通过 block、格网与 LOCO，但只作相关 family 的重复检验，不作为第二机制。NAO 在目标格网中由地理/形态 \(R^2=-0.076\)–\(0.008\) 升至 PC2–PC3 的 \(0.247\)–\(0.299\)；PDO 由 \(0.276\)–\(0.317\) 升至 \(0.445\)–\(0.477\)。PC1 无稳定增益。Niño 3.4 通过 block/格网但去北美 LOCO 失败，不提升；提前一年未重现 PC2–PC3 模式。

This is external climate consistency, not attribution. PCA and sensitivity use different temperature representations and time scales, but both originate from GLAST. Step 10 uses linear detrending and ordinary Pearson correlation without time-series autocorrelation correction. Spatial block CV tests geographic generalization only. Do not claim a unique index, pathway, seasonal mechanism, or causal forcing from this result.

这是外部气候一致性，不是归因。PCA 与敏感性使用不同温度表征和时间尺度，但都来自 GLAST；Step 10 为线性去趋势后的普通 Pearson 相关，未校正时间自相关；空间 block CV 只检验地理泛化。不能据此主张唯一指数、作用路径、季节机制或因果 forcing。
