## Seasonal teleconnection association

The annual screen was not promoted: it loses seasonal phase and gives an ambiguous mix of NAO/AO/PDO associations. A bounded seasonal producer (Step 17) was therefore used only to test the predeclared matching-season, lag-0/lag-1 association fields for NAO, AO, PDO, and Niño 3.4. Each lake series is seasonally linearly detrended; 0 °C all-ice states are excluded; lake correlations are Fisher-\\z\\ transformed and pair-count-weighted within PCA equal-area cells.

> 年尺度筛查不提升为结果：它丢失季节相位，且得到 NAO/AO/PDO 的模糊混合。故仅以 Step 17 做受限季节筛查：匹配季节、滞后 0/1 年、NAO/AO/PDO/Niño 3.4。每湖季节序列线性去趋势，排除 0 °C 全冰状态；逐湖相关 Fisher-\\z\\ 后按配对数汇总至 PCA 等面积格网。

Retained discovery result: JJA LSWT in year \\t\\ versus JJA NAO/AO in \\t-1\\. The response is a spatial lagged seasonal association field, not a causal sensitivity field. The model compares every PC1–PC3 combination after latitude, longitude, elevation, lake area, mean depth, and coast distance are included.

> 保留的发现结果：\\t\\ 年 JJA LSWT 与 \\t-1\\ 年 JJA NAO/AO。响应是空间滞后季节关联场，不是因果敏感性场。模型在纬度、经度、高程、湖面积、平均深度、离海距离后比较 PC1–PC3 的全部组合。

## Result and stability

At reference spatial blocks, NAO held-out \\R^2\\ changes from \\-0.089\\ for geography/morphology to \\0.322\\ for PC2–PC3; AO changes from \\-0.143\\ to \\0.180\\. PC1 alone gives no meaningful gain, PC3 alone does not, and PC1–PC2 / PC1–PC3 are weaker equal-dimensional alternatives. Adding PC1 to PC2–PC3 slightly lowers the result. PC2–PC3 therefore identifies the spatial association field more specifically than the primary common-warming PC1 pattern.

> 参考空间 blocks 中，NAO 留出 \\R^2\\ 由地理/形态的 \\-0.089\\ 升至 PC2–PC3 的 \\0.322\\；AO 由 \\-0.143\\ 升至 \\0.180\\。PC1 单独无实质增益，PC3 单独没有，PC1–PC2/PC1–PC3 作为同维替代更弱；在 PC2–PC3 上再加 PC1 略降。因此 PC2–PC3 比主要共同增温背景 PC1 更特异地定位该空间关联场。

PC2–PC3 increment stays positive across three equal-area grids, every leave-one-continent-out PCA refit, and all four leave-one-decade-out association calculations. Full versus decade-omitted fields have cell Spearman 0.755–0.913 for NAO and 0.818–0.890 for AO. AO is retained only as correlated-family replication; PDO fails LOCO when Asia is omitted and is not promoted.

> PC2–PC3 增益在三种等面积格网、去除任一大洲重拟 PCA、以及四次去年代关联计算中均为正。完整场与去年代场的格网 Spearman：NAO 0.755–0.913，AO 0.818–0.890。AO 只作相关 family 复现；PDO 去亚洲 LOCO 失败，不提升。

## Boundary and next test

The result is external climate consistency, not circulation attribution. PCA and association field are distinct transforms but share GLAST as their temperature source; ordinary correlations have 39–40 seasonal pairs after linear detrending; spatial hold-out tests geographic transfer only. Next analysis should first expand the baseline with already available lake hydrology and morphology (residence time, discharge, watershed area, volume, shoreline development, reservoir status). It tests whether PC2–PC3 remains informative after physically relevant lake context is included, before requesting a large new forcing panel.

> 结果是外部气候一致性，不是环流归因。PCA 与关联场是不同变换，但同源于 GLAST；线性去趋势后普通相关仅有 39–40 个季节配对；空间留出只检验地理迁移。下一步先用已有湖泊水文/形态信息扩展基线：停留时间、流量、流域面积、体积、岸线发育度、水库状态。先检验 PC2–PC3 在物理相关湖泊背景后是否仍有信息，再请求大型 forcing 面板。

## Existing lake-context exclusion

The expanded static context uses log residence time, discharge, watershed area, volume, shoreline development, local slope, and reservoir fraction; `Res_time = -1` and `Slope_100 = -1` are missing. All variants use 568 complete cells. Expanded context does not raise spatial held-out prediction: reference-block NAO is \\-0.115\\, AO is \\-0.146\\. PC2–PC3 after context remains \\0.290\\ for NAO and \\0.183\\ for AO.

> 扩展静态背景包括对数停留时间、流量、流域面积、体积、岸线发育度、局地坡度与水库比例；`Res_time = -1`、`Slope_100 = -1` 为缺失。所有变体使用 568 个完整格网。扩展背景不提高空间留出预测：参考 blocks 中 NAO 为 \\-0.115\\、AO 为 \\-0.146\\；背景后 PC2–PC3 仍为 NAO \\0.290\\、AO \\0.183\\。

The PC2–PC3 increment stays positive at all three grids, every LOCO refit, and every LODO omission after expanded context. Weakest case is NAO with North America omitted: absolute held-out \\R^2=-0.011\\, but increment over expanded context remains \\+0.123\\. This narrows a proxy explanation; it does not establish a dynamic mechanism.

> 扩展背景后，PC2–PC3 增量在三格网、所有 LOCO、所有 LODO 仍为正。最弱为去北美 NAO：绝对留出 \\R^2=-0.011\\，但相对扩展背景增量仍为 \\+0.123\\。它缩小代理解释，不建立动态机制。
