# 2026-04-22

The “hiatus” concept is controversial: Medhaug et al. ([2017](#ref-medhaug2017)) (apparent), Lewandowsky et al. ([2015](#ref-lewandowsky2015)) (nonexistent), Fyfe et al. ([2016](#ref-fyfe2016)) (real but short-term modulation).

So, 1998-2012 is not a good reference period for global warming acceleration.
