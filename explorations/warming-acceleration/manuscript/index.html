Global lake warming follows spatially structured temporal pathways

Manuscript workspace

This directory is the formal-paper layer of the project. It turns the validated evidence in draft/ into a concise manuscript. It does not contain exploratory analysis, durable data preparation, or supplementary sensitivity figures.

本目录是正式论文层：把 draft/ 中已验证的证据收敛为可投稿手稿。不放探索分析、持久数据处理或补充敏感性图。

Central claim

Global lake warming is widespread, but lakes differ not only in the magnitude of warming, but also in its temporal pathway. Spatially balanced PCA identifies a common low-frequency background and recurring secondary timing structures; those structures are spatially organised rather than discrete response types.

全球湖泊增温广泛，但差异不只在幅度，也在时间路径。空间平衡 PCA 识别共同低频背景与可重复的次级时间结构；它们具有空间组织，不是离散响应类型。

Agreed writing workflow

The manuscript now follows a fixed order. First, literature evidence is recorded and audited in paper collection and the accompanying paper notes; each claim in the Introduction must map to a specific, read source and its scope. Second, the four core Results sections are written around the retained figures: global kinematics, spatially balanced trajectory structure, stability, and the circumscribed teleconnection association. Methods are then tightened to serve those results, followed by the Discussion. Seasonal and ice diagnostics remain a modular descriptive branch, not a competing causal storyline. Further analysis is added only when it directly resolves a stated evidence gap.

手稿写作顺序现已固定：先在 paper collection 与论文笔记中记录、核验文献证据，Introduction 的每项论断必须对应已阅读文献及其适用范围；再围绕保留图件写四个核心 Results：全球运动学、空间平衡路径结构、稳定性、以及有限的遥相关关联；之后再收紧 Methods，最后写 Discussion。季节与冰保持模块化描述性支路，不发展为竞争性的因果叙事。后续分析只有在直接补足已定义证据缺口时才新增。

Reading order

  1. Introduction
  2. Methods
  3. Results
  4. Discussion and conclusions

Working abstract

Lake surface water temperature is rising worldwide, yet long-term trends alone cannot describe whether lakes warm through similar temporal pathways. Using 1981–2020 reconstructed daily temperatures for 92,245 lakes, we quantify long-term warming and endpoint-aligned decadal warming rates from raw annual LSWT, then use spatially balanced PCA to describe low-frequency trajectories. Warming is widespread, but local decadal rates differ through time and space. The trajectory space contains a dominant common background and recurring secondary timing structures, rather than a small set of discrete lake types. The secondary structures co-locate with heterogeneous sensitivity of summer lake temperature to prior-summer NAO/AO. These results describe reproducible spatial associations and temporal pathways; they do not establish causal drivers.

湖泊表层水温全球升高，但长期趋势不能说明湖泊是否沿相同时间路径增温。本文基于 92,245 个湖泊 1981–2020 年重建日温度，以 raw 年均 LSWT 量化长期增温和端点对齐十年速度，再用空间平衡 PCA 描述低频轨迹。增温广泛，但局地十年速度在时间与空间上不同。轨迹空间包含共同背景与可重复次级时间结构，而非少数离散湖泊类型。次级结构与上一年夏季 NAO/AO 对当年夏季湖温敏感性的空间异质性共定位；这描述可重复关联，不建立因果驱动。
