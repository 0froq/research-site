# Exploratory PC2–PC3 Rotation: Why It Is Not Active Evidence

## Status

This note preserves an exploratory diagnostic that was removed from active Chapter 2 evidence. It must not be cited as a scientific result of the manuscript.

> 本页保留一个已退出 Ch2 主证据的探索诊断。它不得作为论文科学结果引用。

## Diagnostic

PC2 and PC3 exchange rank in some leave-one-continent-out refits. An exploratory analysis regressed raw warming-speed change on their two cell scores and used the fitted coefficient vector to rotate their score plane. The fitted direction was stable across some grids and resamples.

> 部分 LOCO 中 PC2、PC3 会交换排序。探索分析曾用 raw 增温速度变化回归其两个格网 score，再按拟合系数旋转 score 平面；该方向在部分格网与重采样中稳定。

## Why it is excluded

The rotation is selected after observing the same temperature-derived response it is designed to describe. Any two-dimensional PCA plane can be linearly projected onto a direction that maximizes association with a chosen target. Alternative speed descriptors also derive from the same source sequence. Grid and hold-out stability show that this fitted association does not vanish under those resamples; they do not establish a new independent component or physical process.

> 该旋转在观察到同一温度派生响应后才被选出，故属于 post hoc 拟合。任意二维 PCA 平面都可线性投影到与指定目标最相关的方向；替代速度 descriptor 也来自同一源序列。格网与留出稳定只说明该拟合关系未在这些重采样中消失，不能证明新独立成分或物理过程。

The active route therefore keeps PC2–PC3 as a rank-exchanging secondary subspace and tests it only with predeclared external predictor families. See [External Interpretation of Spatially Balanced PCA](../../../explorations/warming-acceleration/prose/pca-external-interpretation.llms.md).

> 当前路线将 PC2–PC3 保留为可交换排序的次级子空间，仅以预先定义的外部 predictor family 检验；见 External Interpretation of Spatially Balanced PCA。
